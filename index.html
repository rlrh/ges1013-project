Instructions

This game is divided into 3 parts. Using information and clues given in this website (chat bot), you will be guided to explore 6 different locations.

In the 1st half of the game, you will visit 3 sites virtually. This means that you can play the game at any time and place. You will also need access to the internet to help you complete the tasks in the game.

In the 2nd half of the game,you will need to visit the other 3 sites in person. Information will still be provided in the chatbot to guide you. In addition to using the internet, you need to physically explore the sites to search for clues and to complete tasks. The travelling time between each site is around 15 to 25 mins by bus or 5 to 10 mins by car.

You do not have to complete the game at one go as your progress will be auto-saved after you complete each location. This will be indicated by this message: “Your progress has been saved.” Do note that you will have to use the same device.


Good luck and we hope that you enjoy the game!
